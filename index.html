Learning and Dynamical Systems:
Theory and Applications

Thursday, June 4, 2026  ·  13:00–17:00 @ Harry Nyquist room, Department of Decision and Control Systems, KTH

About

This one-day workshop brings together researchers working at the intersection of machine learning and dynamical systems. Talks will cover recent theoretical advances as well as applications across control, robotics, and the physical sciences. The event is hosted by the Department of Decision and Control Systems at KTH.
